ABOUT TAGWORLD

// WALLS, WORDS, AND THE WORLD //

🌏

What is TAGWORLD?

TAGWORLD is a web platform that bridges Southeast Asian graffiti culture with the UN Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs). It is a space for artists, youth, and communities to create, share, and learn — using graffiti as a lens for understanding urban sustainability and responsible creative practice.

🎨

Our Mission

To amplify the voices of Southeast Asian graffiti artists, document their stories, and explore how street art contributes to sustainable cities (SDG 11) and responsible production (SDG 12). We believe graffiti is not vandalism — it is culture, community, and a form of urban language.

🤝

Our Collaborators

Conflict X — Malang City, East Java, Indonesia. A graffiti collective embodying hip-hop, DIY ethos, and urban pride. Their brand and story form the heart of our artist spotlight series.


We also feature and collaborate with artists from the Philippines, Indonesia, Japan, and Taiwan — building bridges across Southeast and East Asian graffiti scenes.

📖

The SDG Framework

SDG 11 — Sustainable Cities & Communities: Graffiti revitalizes public spaces, gives youth a platform for self-expression, and shapes inclusive urban visual identity.


SDG 12 — Responsible Consumption & Production: Graffiti artists often work with minimal materials, recycle resources, and operate outside industrial production — a naturally sustainable creative economy.
